Research

My research focuses on reconstructing thin, sparse 3D structures — vascular networks in medical CT, industrial pipe systems — where conventional surface and volumetric methods tend to over-smooth or break topology. More recently I've been working on controlled diffusion models for capacity-constrained point-set generation.

Code for several projects is available on GitHub.
